    Membership

    You can learn more about the organization and its structure through the GLOS Bylaws (April 2021) or reading the most up to date Membership Flyer (pdf).

    To join, you may send the Membership Form (pdf) along with payment the address listed on the bottom of the form.

    You can also find the club on social media. We have a presence on Facebook through our public group page Greater Lansing Orchid Society.

    If you are still not convinced membership is for you, you are always more than welcome to find a meeting to to attend on our Calendar and check it out.
