    2026 Greater Lansing Orchid Society Show and Sale

    Each year, during the last weekend in February, the Greater Lansing Orchid Society sponsors an orchid show and sale. This year's show will take place February 28 through March 1

    2026 Show Specifics

    • Dates:February 28 - March 1, 2026
    • Location Van Attas Greenhouse and Flower Shop
    • Show Chair: Becky Thayer
    • Art Show Chair: Carol Ann Stuht
    • Vendor Contact: Pete Ostlund
    • AOS Juding Coordinator:Doris Asher (517-332-0004)

    Events Happening During the Show

    • Orchid Displays: various exhibits of orchids grown by members of regional orchid societies.
    • Orchid Sale: One of the larget orchid sales featuring vendors from across the Midwest
    • Orchid Art Show and Sale: a jurored Orchid Art Show with works by talented artists
    • Floral Arrangement Show: presented by Ikebana International Lansing Chapter #134
    • Educational Classes: Society members provide free workshops on orchid care
    • Raffle: A raffle will be held on Sunday. Tickets are $1 each or 6 for $5.
      Winners will be drawn at 3:00 PM on Sunday. You do not need to be present to win.
    • Orchid Judging: American Orchid Society judges will be present to judge the orchids on display.
      Judging will take place on Saturday morning, and results are posted in the afternoon.
    • Photography: Special photography hours before the show opens on Sunday. Tripods are welcome only at this time.
